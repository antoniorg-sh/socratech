I. Colophon

I am Antonio. A computer scientist working in AI infrastructure and distributed systems. My weeks are at Google; my evenings go to Operand, the company I am building, and to Socratech, where the thinking is worked out in public. My subjects — AI, systems, cryptography, and the mathematics beneath all three — are separate chapters of one question: what is worth knowing deeply when the machines can do most of the rest.

This site keeps three registers. How it works — from first principles: protocols, tensors, the arithmetic beneath cryptography, the quiet mechanics people use without ever reading. What is happening — the honest read on AI and the bargain it is asking of engineers. What I am building — Operand, a mathematics relearned from scratch, the slow apprenticeship of learning deeply in an age that rewards the shallow and the fast. The longer account of how I arrived here is kept in the timeline.
